Japanese Curry

Description

Japanese curry is a dish that is enjoyed by many people in Japan. It is a dish that is made with a variety of ingredients, including meat, vegetables, and spices. The dish is typically served with rice and is a popular choice for lunch or dinner.

There are many different variations of Japanese curry, with some recipes calling for beef, chicken, or pork. The dish is typically made by simmering the meat and vegetables in a curry sauce that is made from a roux, which is a mixture of flour, butter, and curry powder. The dish is then served over rice and is often garnished with pickles or other condiments.

Japanese curry is a comforting and satisfying dish that is enjoyed by people of all ages. It is a popular choice for lunch or dinner and is often served in restaurants and homes throughout Japan. The dish is easy to make and can be customized to suit your taste preferences, making it a versatile and delicious meal option.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, chopped
  • 1 pound chicken cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 box curry cube
  • cooked rice, for serving

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, carrots, and potatoes and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add water and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Add curry cube and stir until dissolved.
  7. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  8. Serve over rice.

Enjoy your delicious Japanese curry!
