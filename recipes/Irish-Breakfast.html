Irish Breakfast

Description

An Irish breakfast is a traditional breakfast dish in Ireland. It typically consists of sausages, bacon, eggs, tomatoes, black pudding, white pudding, and toast. It is a hearty and filling meal that is perfect for starting the day.

Ingredients

  • 2 sausages
  • 2 rashers of bacon
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 egg
  • 1 slice of black pudding
  • 1 slice of white pudding
  • 1 slice of toast
  • 1 knob of butter

Steps

  1. Heat the grill to high.
  2. Grill the sausages and bacon for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through and golden.
  3. Meanwhile, heat a non-stick frying pan over a medium heat.
  4. Halve the tomato and cook, cut-side down, for 5 minutes until softened and slightly charred.
  5. Remove and set aside.
  6. Crack the egg into the pan and fry for 2-3 minutes until the white is set and the yolk is still runny.
  7. Remove and set aside.
  8. Heat a non-stick frying pan over a medium heat.
